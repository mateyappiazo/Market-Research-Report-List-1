<p><ul><li>Acid-based Staining</li><li>Water-based Staining</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The stained concrete market primarily includes acid-based and water-based staining types. Acid-based staining utilizes metallic salts to generate vibrant, translucent color effects that enhance the concrete's texture and depth, making it ideal for indoor and outdoor applications. In contrast, water-based staining provides a wide range of color options and is easier to apply, offering a faster drying time and reduced odor. Both methods are popular for achieving aesthetically pleasing surfaces, with applications in residential, commercial, and industrial settings.</p></p>
