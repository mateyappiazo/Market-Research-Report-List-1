<p><p>Roll groovers are essential tools for creating grooves in pipes, facilitating the installation of pipe couplings. The market is divided into two primary types: hydraulic and manual. Hydraulic roll groovers are powered by hydraulic systems, enabling quick and efficient operation with minimal effort, ideal for heavy-duty tasks. In contrast, manual roll groovers rely on physical strength for operation, making them suitable for smaller jobs or areas with limited space. Both types cater to different user needs and operational conditions in various industries.</p></p>
