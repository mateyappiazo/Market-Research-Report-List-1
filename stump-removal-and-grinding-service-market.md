<p><ul><li>Removal</li><li>Grinding</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Stump removal and grinding services cater to two primary market types: removal and grinding. Stump removal involves excavating the entire stump, including roots, providing a clean slate for landscaping or construction. In contrast, stump grinding involves cutting the stump down to below ground level using specialized equipment, leaving wood chips behind. Both services are essential for improving property aesthetics and safety, with removal being more invasive and thorough, while grinding is less disruptive and often more cost-effective.</p></p>
