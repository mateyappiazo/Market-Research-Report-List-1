<p><p>The intravascular ultrasound (IVUS) market includes two primary types: platform intravascular ultrasound and compact intravascular ultrasound. Platform IVUS systems are typically larger, full-featured devices often used in hospitals for extensive cardiac applications, providing detailed imaging for complex cases. In contrast, compact IVUS systems are smaller, portable devices designed for ease of use and quick imaging in various clinical settings. Both types enable enhanced visualization of vascular structures, aiding in diagnostics and interventional procedures.</p></p>
