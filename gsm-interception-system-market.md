<p><ul><li>Active Interception System</li><li>Passive Interception System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The GSM interception system market comprises two main types: active and passive interception systems. Active interception systems involve direct engagement with mobile networks to capture communications in real-time, often utilizing techniques that allow for full access to conversations and data transfer. In contrast, passive interception systems monitor and collect signals without altering the network, focusing on channel analysis and data extraction without interference. Both types cater to various sectors, including law enforcement and intelligence, emphasizing security and surveillance applications.</p></p>
