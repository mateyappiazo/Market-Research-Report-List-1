<p><p>The radio frequency skin tightening machine market is primarily categorized into two types: unipolar and bipolar. Unipolar devices deliver focused energy from a single electrode, penetrating deeper layers of the skin, making them suitable for more extensive skin tightening. In contrast, bipolar devices use two electrodes, providing more controlled energy delivery to the surface, which is ideal for treating localized areas and enhancing skin texture. Both types cater to various aesthetic needs, driving demand in the beauty and wellness industry.</p></p>
