<p><strong>The Home Nebulizer Therapy Device Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>"Compression Nebulizer"</li><li>"Ultrasonic Nebulizer"</li><li>"Mesh Nebulizer"</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Home nebulizer therapy devices are categorized into three main types: compression nebulizers, ultrasonic nebulizers, and mesh nebulizers. Compression nebulizers use compressed air to convert liquid medication into aerosol droplets for inhalation, making them effective for a range of respiratory conditions. Ultrasonic nebulizers utilize ultrasonic vibrations to create a fine mist, offering a quieter and more portable option. Mesh nebulizers use a vibrating mesh to produce aerosol, ensuring efficient medication delivery and minimal residual volume, ideal for patients needing precise dosages.</p></p>
