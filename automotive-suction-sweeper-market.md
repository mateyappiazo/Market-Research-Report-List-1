<p><ul><li>Walk-Behind Suction Sweepers</li><li>Ride-On Suction Sweepers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive suction sweeper market comprises two main types: walk-behind and ride-on suction sweepers. Walk-behind suction sweepers are manually operated, designed for smaller areas, providing enhanced maneuverability and environmental benefits. They are ideal for sidewalks, parking lots, and tight spaces. In contrast, ride-on suction sweepers are larger, designed for extensive surfaces like roads, airports, and industrial areas. These machines offer greater efficiency and speed, allowing operators to cover larger areas with minimal labor, making them suitable for municipal and commercial applications.</p></p>
