<p><ul><li>Wafer Tester</li><li>Packaged Device Tester</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Analog IC Tester market is primarily divided into two segments: Wafer Tester and Packaged Device Tester. Wafer Testers evaluate integrated circuits while still on the silicon wafer, focusing on performance and yield before packaging. In contrast, Packaged Device Testers assess fully manufactured analog ICs, ensuring they meet specifications and functionality. Both types are crucial for the semiconductor industry, as they help identify defects, enhance product quality, and optimize manufacturing processes, thereby ensuring reliability and performance in various applications.</p></p>
