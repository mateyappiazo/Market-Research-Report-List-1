<p><p>The Aerospace Thermal Management System market features two primary types: Air-to-Air Thermal Transfer Type and Air-to-Liquid Thermal Transfer Type. The Air-to-Air type utilizes airflow to dissipate heat between components, enhancing efficiency without complex liquid systems. In contrast, the Air-to-Liquid type employs a fluid medium to absorb and transfer heat, offering greater thermal control in high-performance environments. Both types are essential for maintaining optimal temperatures in aerospace applications, ensuring safety and operational efficiency in various aircraft systems.</p></p>
