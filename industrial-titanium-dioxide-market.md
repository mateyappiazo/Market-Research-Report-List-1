<p><strong>The Industrial Titanium Dioxide Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Sulfuric Acid Industrial Titanium Dioxide</li><li>Chlorination Industrial Titanium Dioxide</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Industrial Titanium Dioxide market is primarily categorized into two types based on production methods: Sulfuric Acid and Chlorination processes. Sulfuric Acid Industrial Titanium Dioxide is produced using sulfuric acid to extract titanium from ilmenite, resulting in a high-quality pigment favored for its brightness and opacity. In contrast, Chlorination Industrial Titanium Dioxide is derived from titanium tetrachloride through a chlorination reaction, offering superior purity and is often used in high-performance applications like aerospace and automotive industries.</p></p>
