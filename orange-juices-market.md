<p><p>The orange juice market includes several types: </p><p>1. **100% Fruit Juice**: Pure juice with no additives, offering optimal flavor and nutrition. </p><p>2. **Nectars**: Made from fruit pulp and water, often sweetened, providing a thicker consistency. </p><p>3. **Juice Drinks**: Typically diluted with water and sweeteners, these offer a lower fruit content and are more affordable. </p><p>4. **Concentrates**: Dehydrated juice, requiring reconstitution with water, offering convenience and longer shelf life. </p><p>5. **Powdered Juice**: Powdered form, easily mixed with water for quick preparation and portability. </p></p>
