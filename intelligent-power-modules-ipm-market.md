<p><p>Intelligent Power Modules (IPMs) are advanced semiconductor devices that integrate power electronics and control circuits, enhancing efficiency and reliability in various applications. The IGBT-IPM market focuses on insulated gate bipolar transistors, ideal for high-power applications like motor drives and renewable energy systems. In contrast, the MOSFET-IPM market revolves around metal-oxide-semiconductor field-effect transistors, suited for high-frequency and low-to-medium power applications. Both types offer benefits such as compact design, thermal performance, and simplified circuit integration, catering to diverse industries.</p></p>
