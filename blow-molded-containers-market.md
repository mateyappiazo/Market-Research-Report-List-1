<p><p>Blow molded containers are primarily categorized by the type of plastic used. PET (Polyethylene Terephthalate) is favored for its clarity and recyclability, commonly in beverage bottles. HDPE (High-Density Polyethylene) offers robust strength and is used for milk and detergent containers. Polypropylene provides flexibility, ideal for food containers. Polystyrene is lightweight and often used in disposable items. PVC (Polyvinyl Chloride) is versatile, though less common for food. LDPE (Low-Density Polyethylene) is flexible and used in bags. Polycarbonates are strong thermoplastics for durable containers. Other materials may include various specialty plastics and biodegradable options.</p></p>
