<p><ul><li>Electric Motor Driven Pump</li><li>Direct Drive Pump</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Aerospace hydraulic pumps are essential for powering hydraulic systems in aircraft. The market is primarily divided into two types: electric motor-driven pumps and direct drive pumps. Electric motor-driven pumps utilize electric motors to generate hydraulic pressure, offering advantages like precise control and reduced noise. In contrast, direct drive pumps are mechanically linked to aircraft engines, providing high reliability and efficiency at the cost of greater weight and complexity. Both types play critical roles in modern aerospace applications, each serving specific operational needs.</p></p>
