<p><strong>The PU Catalysts Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Reactive Amine Catalysts</li><li>Non-reactive Amine Catalysts</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The PU catalysts market is segmented into reactive and non-reactive amine catalysts. Reactive amine catalysts actively participate in the polyurethane formation process, promoting gelation and enhancing curing speed, essential for products requiring rapid hardening. Non-reactive amine catalysts, on the other hand, do not chemically bond with the polyurethane but instead accelerate the reaction without becoming part of the final product. Each type serves distinct applications in industries such as coatings, foams, and elastomers, catering to various performance needs.</p></p>
