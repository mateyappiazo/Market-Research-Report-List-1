<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Cerebral Infarction Therapy Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Partial Anterior Circulation Infarct (PACI)</li><li>Total Anterior Circulation Infarct (TACI)</li><li>Posterior Circulation Infarct (POCI)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Cerebral Infarction Therapy market is categorized into three main types based on the affected brain regions. Partial Anterior Circulation Infarct (PACI) involves limited areas of the anterior circulation, leading to partial neurological deficits. Total Anterior Circulation Infarct (TACI) affects broader areas, causing severe and widespread functional impairments. Posterior Circulation Infarct (POCI) affects the brainstem and cerebellum, resulting in distinct symptoms like dizziness or balance issues. Each type requires tailored therapeutic approaches for effective management and recovery.</p></p>
