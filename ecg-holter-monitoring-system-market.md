<p><p>The ECG Holter Monitoring System market comprises various types based on lead configurations. The 12 Lead ECG provides comprehensive cardiac analysis, capturing detailed electrical activity. Wireless ECG systems enhance convenience by transmitting data without physical connections, promoting patient mobility and comfort during monitoring sessions.
