<p><p>The Erbium-Doped Fiber market is primarily categorized into two types: Single-Mode (SM) Fiber and Polarization-Maintaining (PM) Fiber. SM Fiber is widely used for long-distance communication due to its low loss and high bandwidth capabilities. In contrast, PM Fiber is designed to maintain the polarization of light, making it essential for applications requiring high performance and stability, such as quantum communication and fiber optic sensing. Both types play critical roles in enhancing optical network efficiency and performance.</p></p>
