<p><ul><li>Heat Pump Systems</li><li>Heat Recovery Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Variable Refrigerant Flow (VRF) system market comprises two main types: heat pump systems and heat recovery systems. Heat pump systems are designed to either heat or cool spaces by transferring refrigerant to indoor units as needed, making them versatile for various climates. In contrast, heat recovery systems efficiently provide simultaneous heating and cooling to different areas by redirecting excess heat from one zone to another, enhancing energy efficiency and comfort in larger buildings.</p></p>
