<p><p>Ticketing systems can be categorized into three main market types: On-Premise, Web-Based, and Cloud-Based. On-Premise systems are installed locally on a company's servers, offering complete control but requiring maintenance. Web-Based solutions are accessible through browsers, providing flexibility and ease of use without local installation. Cloud-Based systems are hosted on the service provider's servers, allowing for scalability, automatic updates, and remote access, making them ideal for businesses seeking cost-effective and efficient ticket management without hardware investment.</p></p>
