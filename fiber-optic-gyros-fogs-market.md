<p><strong>The Fiber Optic Gyros (FOGs) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Interferometric FOG</li><li>Resonant FOG</li><li>Stimulated Brillouin Scattering FOG</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Fiber Optic Gyros (FOGs) are advanced sensors that utilize light propagation through optical fibers to measure angular velocity. The market for FOGs is categorized into three types: Interferometric FOGs, which use interference patterns to detect changes in light phases; Resonant FOGs, which rely on the resonance of light within a fiber loop for precise measurements; and Stimulated Brillouin Scattering FOGs, which leverage the scattering of light to enhance sensitivity and performance. Each type serves specific applications in navigation and stabilization systems.</p></p>
