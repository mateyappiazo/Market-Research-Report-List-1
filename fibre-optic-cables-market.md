<p><strong>The Fibre Optic Cables Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single-mode Optical Cable</li><li>Multimode Optical Cable</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fiber optic cables market is primarily segmented into two types: single-mode and multimode optical cables. Single-mode cables feature a small core diameter, allowing only one light mode to propagate, making them ideal for long-distance communication with minimal signal loss. In contrast, multimode cables have a larger core diameter, supporting multiple light modes, which facilitates shorter-distance transmission and higher bandwidth in local networks. Both types are essential for modern telecommunications, catering to diverse connectivity needs across various applications.</p></p>
