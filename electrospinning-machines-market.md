<p><p>The electrospinning machines market includes various types, each catering to specific applications. Coaxial electrospinning allows for the simultaneous production of two distinct fibers, enabling core-shell structures useful in drug delivery and sensor technologies. Emulsion electrospinning involves dispersing polymer solutions in emulsions, creating fibers with enhanced properties and functionality. Melt electrospinning uses molten polymers, eliminating solvents and simplifying production, making it ideal for industrial-scale applications. Together, these technologies expand the range of material properties and functional uses in various industries.</p></p>
