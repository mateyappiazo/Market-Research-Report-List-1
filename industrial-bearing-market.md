<p><p>The industrial bearing market includes various types, each serving specific applications. Spherical roller bearings (SRB) handle high radial and axial loads with misalignment tolerance. Deep groove ball bearings (DGBB) offer versatility and efficiency in various machinery. Cylindrical roller bearings (CRB) manage heavy loads and high speeds. Double-row tapered roller bearings (DTRB) support combined loads in compact designs. Spherical thrust roller bearings (STRB) facilitate high axial loads, while angular contact ball bearings (ACBB) enable precision in high-speed applications. Additionally, "Others" covers specialized or niche bearings.</p></p>
