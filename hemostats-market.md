<p><p>The hemostats market comprises various types designed to control bleeding during surgical procedures. Thrombin-based hemostats use recombinant thrombin to promote clot formation. Oxidized regenerated cellulose-based hemostats facilitate healing through a cellulose matrix that enhances coagulation. Combination hemostats integrate multiple components for improved efficacy. Gelatin-based hemostats absorb moisture and form a gel-like structure to support hemostasis. Collagen-based hemostats leverage collagen's natural properties to accelerate clot formation and tissue repair. Each type serves distinct clinical needs.</p></p>
