<p><strong>The Food Grade Silicone Oil Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>H1</li><li>H2</li><li>H3</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Food grade silicone oil is classified into three primary types: H1, H2, and H3. H1 silicone oil is considered food-safe for incidental contact and is often used as a release agent or lubricant in food processing equipment. H2 silicone oil is used in non-food contact applications, offering safety margins for environments where accidental exposure may occur. H3 silicone oil, while also food-safe, is specifically formulated for direct contact in food applications, providing higher performance in cooking and baking processes.</p></p>
