<p><p>The automobile exhaust muffler market is categorized by several types based on their inlet and outlet configurations. Center inlet mufflers feature a single inlet at the center and typically have a center outlet, optimizing space and exhaust flow. Dual inlet mufflers have two inlets, allowing for better performance in high-powered vehicles. Offset inlet and offset outlet mufflers provide unique configurations to accommodate specific vehicle designs. Each type enhances sound attenuation and exhaust efficiency according to vehicle requirements and configurations.</p></p>
