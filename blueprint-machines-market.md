<p><ul><li>Ammonia Print Machine</li><li>Ammonia Free Print Machine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Blueprint machines are categorized into two main market types: ammonia print machines and ammonia-free print machines. Ammonia print machines utilize ammonium-based solutions for developing blueprints, offering high-quality and detailed reproductions. In contrast, ammonia-free print machines employ environmentally friendly chemicals, eliminating harmful emissions associated with ammonia. This shift not only caters to eco-conscious consumers but also meets regulatory standards. Both types serve the architectural and engineering sectors, providing versatile options based on quality, safety, and environmental impact preferences.</p></p>
