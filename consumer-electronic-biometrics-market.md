<p><ul><li>Capacitive Sensors</li><li>Optical Sensors</li><li>Electric Field Sensors</li><li>Thermal Sensors</li><li>Ultrasound Sensors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The consumer electronic biometrics market includes various sensor types. Capacitive sensors detect touch by measuring changes in capacitance, widely used in fingerprint recognition. Optical sensors capture images of fingerprints or facial features, relying on light to create detailed biometric data. Electric field sensors use electric fields to measure biometric traits, like veins in a finger. Thermal sensors detect heat patterns, useful for face recognition. Lastly, ultrasound sensors emit sound waves to capture detailed images of biometric features, enhancing accuracy in identification.</p></p>
