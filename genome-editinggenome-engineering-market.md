<p><strong>The Genome Editing/Genome Engineering Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>CRISPR</li><li>TALEN</li><li>ZFN</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The genome editing market comprises three primary technologies: CRISPR, TALEN, and ZFN. CRISPR (Clustered Regularly Interspaced Short Palindromic Repeats) is a versatile and cost-effective method that allows precise modification of DNA. TALEN (Transcription Activator-Like Effector Nucleases) offers specificity and is suitable for targeted gene manipulation. ZFN (Zinc Finger Nucleases) utilizes zinc finger proteins to bind and edit DNA sequences but is more complex and less commonly used than the other methods. Each technology has unique applications and implications in research and therapeutics.</p></p>
