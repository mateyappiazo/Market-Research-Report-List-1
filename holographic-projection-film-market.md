<p><p>The holographic projection film market comprises two primary types: front projection film and rear projection film. Front projection film is designed for installation where the projector is positioned in front of the screen, allowing for vibrant displays but requiring controlled lighting conditions. Rear projection film, on the other hand, is installed at the back of the display surface, enabling brighter and clearer images from ambient light and providing flexibility in design and placement. Both types cater to varying visual display needs across industries.</p></p>
