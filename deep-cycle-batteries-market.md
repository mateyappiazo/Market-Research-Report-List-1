<p><strong>The Deep Cycle Batteries Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>VRLA</li><li>FLA</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The deep cycle battery market is primarily categorized into two types: Valve Regulated Lead Acid (VRLA) and Flooded Lead Acid (FLA) batteries. VRLA batteries are sealed and maintenance-free, offering safety and convenience, making them popular in renewable energy systems and UPS applications. In contrast, FLA batteries require regular maintenance and are more susceptible to spillage, but they provide higher durability and deeper discharge capabilities, often used in industrial applications and renewable energy storage solutions. Both types serve distinct functionalities within the market.</p></p>
