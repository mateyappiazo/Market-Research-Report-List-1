<p><ul><li> Shaft Seal Diaphragm Structure</li><li>Flexible Tube Structure</li><li>Torsional Tube Structure</li><li>Differential Pressure Target Structure </li></ul></p>
<p><p>The cryogenic target flow meter market encompasses various types based on structural design, each suited for specific applications. The shaft seal diaphragm structure is designed for high-pressure environments, ensuring minimal leakage. The flexible tube structure accommodates thermal expansions, ideal for fluctuating temperatures. The torsional tube structure offers high accuracy in fluid measurement by utilizing rotational dynamics. Lastly, the differential pressure target structure leverages pressure differences to determine flow rates, making it versatile in various cryogenic applications.</p></p>
