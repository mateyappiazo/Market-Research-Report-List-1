<p><p>The High-Speed Coupling market includes several types designed for specific applications. Flexible Elastic Couplings accommodate misalignments while providing shock absorption, ideal for varied loads. Torsionally-rigid Flexible Couplings transmit torque without distortion, ensuring precision in high-speed machinery. Rigid Couplings connect shafts directly, offering minimal backlash for consistent performance. Flange Couplings feature robust flanges for easy installation, supporting high-speed rotation with stability. Each type serves distinct functions to enhance efficiency and reliability in mechanical systems.</p></p>
