<p><ul><li>Mammalian Protein Expression Systems</li><li>Baculovirus Protein Expression Systems</li><li>Prokaryotic Protein Expression Systems</li><li>Yeast Protein Expression Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Protein Expression Technology market comprises various systems for synthesizing proteins. Mammalian protein expression systems are favored for producing complex proteins akin to human proteins, essential for therapeutic applications. Baculovirus systems utilize insect cells for high-yield protein production, suitable for eukaryotic post-translational modifications. Prokaryotic systems, typically using bacteria, are cost-effective for simpler proteins and rapid production. Yeast protein expression systems offer a middle ground, enabling post-translational modifications while maintaining relatively low costs and handling eukaryotic proteins effectively.</p></p>
