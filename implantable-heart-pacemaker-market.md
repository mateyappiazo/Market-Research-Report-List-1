<p><p>The implantable heart pacemaker market is categorized into temporary and permanent types. Temporary pacemakers are designed for short-term use in critical care settings, providing immediate support during acute cardiac conditions. They are usually external and can be easily removed. In contrast, permanent pacemakers are surgically implanted for long-term heart rate management in patients with chronic arrhythmias. These devices are designed to be durable and are typically equipped with advanced features to monitor and regulate heart function over time.</p></p>
