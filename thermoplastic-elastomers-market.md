<p><p>The thermoplastic elastomers (TPE) market comprises several key types. Styrene-based TPEs (SBCs) are known for their versatility and strength, commonly used in adhesives and sealants. Thermoplastic polyolefins (TPO) offer excellent weather resistance and are widely utilized in automotive applications. Thermoplastic polyurethanes (TPU) provide outstanding abrasion resistance and flexibility, making them ideal for footwear and medical devices. Polyether ester TPEs (TPEE) combine durability and elasticity, suitable for various consumer products. Other types include specialty blends and innovative formulations, expanding application possibilities.</p></p>
