<p><ul><li>Shell and Tube Heat Exchangers</li><li>Plate Heat Exchangers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>In the ship heat exchangers market, two primary types are shell and tube heat exchangers and plate heat exchangers. Shell and tube heat exchangers consist of a series of tubes, allowing heat transfer between two fluids while maintaining their separation, making them durable and efficient for high-pressure applications. Plate heat exchangers, on the other hand, utilize thin plates to maximize surface area, enhancing heat transfer efficiency in compact designs. Both types are essential for temperature control in marine systems, improving energy efficiency and operational performance.</p></p>
