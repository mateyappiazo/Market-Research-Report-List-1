<p><p>The high-power lasers market comprises three main types: CO2 lasers, solid-state lasers, and fiber lasers. CO2 lasers are gas-based and effective for cutting, engraving, and industrial applications. Solid-state lasers use semiconductor diodes and are known for their efficiency and compact size, often used in medical and defense sectors. Fiber lasers, utilizing optical fibers for amplification, are favored for their high power, precision, and versatility in applications ranging from manufacturing to telecommunications, driving growth across various industries.</p></p>
