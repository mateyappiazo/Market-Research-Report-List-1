<p><p>The torque wrench pump market encompasses three primary types: manual driven, air driven, and electric driven. Manual driven pumps rely on human strength to achieve the required torque, offering simplicity and affordability. Air driven pumps utilize compressed air to create torque, making them faster and suitable for high-volume applications. Electric driven pumps provide precise torque control, enhanced efficiency, and are ideal for automated processes, catering to industries requiring consistent and reliable performance in fastening and assembly tasks.</p></p>
