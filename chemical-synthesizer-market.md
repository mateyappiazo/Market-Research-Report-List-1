<p><ul><li>Single Mode</li><li>Parallel Mode</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The chemical synthesizer market is categorized into two primary modes: single mode and parallel mode. Single mode synthesizers operate one reaction at a time, allowing for precise control and optimization of individual reactions, suitable for small-scale production. In contrast, parallel mode synthesizers enable simultaneous execution of multiple reactions, significantly increasing throughput and efficiency, ideal for large-scale production or screening reactions. Each mode serves distinct applications, catering to varying production needs and research demands in the chemical industry.</p></p>
