<p><p>The PE foam tape market is divided into two main types: double-sided and single-sided. Double-sided foam tape features adhesive on both sides, making it ideal for bonding two surfaces together, providing strong adhesion without visible fasteners. It is commonly used in applications such as mounting, insulation, and sealing. Single-sided foam tape has adhesive on one side, suitable for applications requiring a secure bond to one surface, often used for cushioning, mounting, and surface protection. Both types offer versatility and durability.</p></p>
