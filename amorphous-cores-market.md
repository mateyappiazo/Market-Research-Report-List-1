The market for these cores primarily includes toroidal cores, known for their compact design and low magnetic leakage, making them ideal for transformers and inductors. Cut cores, on the other hand, are constructed from laminated sheets of amorphous material, providing flexibility in design while maintaining low core losses. Both types cater to industries focused on energy efficiency and high-performance magnetic solutions.</p></p>
