<p><p>The blocked isocyanate market is categorized into two primary types: water-based blocked isocyanate and solvent-based blocked isocyanate. Water-based blocked isocyanates are environmentally friendly alternatives, offering lower volatile organic compound emissions, making them suitable for eco-conscious applications such as coatings and adhesives. In contrast, solvent-based blocked isocyanates provide enhanced performance in terms of adhesion and durability, often used in industrial settings. Both types cater to diverse industry needs while emphasizing the balance between performance and environmental considerations.</p></p>
