<p><ul><li>Windshield Projected HUD</li><li>Combiner Projected HUD</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Automotive Head-Up Display (HUD) market consists of two main types: Windshield Projected HUD and Combiner Projected HUD. Windshield Projected HUD projects information directly onto the vehicle's windshield, allowing drivers to see essential data, such as speed and navigation, without looking away from the road. In contrast, Combiner Projected HUD utilizes a small transparent screen that sits above the dashboard to display similar information. Both systems enhance driving safety by minimizing distractions and improving situational awareness.</p></p>
