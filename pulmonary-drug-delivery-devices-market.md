<p><ul><li>Dry Powder Inhaler</li><li>Metered Dose Inhaler</li><li>Nebulizer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The pulmonary drug delivery devices market includes various types designed for respiratory therapy. Dry Powder Inhalers (DPIs) deliver medication as a fine powder, activated by the patient's inhalation effort, allowing for deeper lung penetration. Metered Dose Inhalers (MDIs) dispense aerosolized medication in predetermined doses, utilizing a propellant for delivery. Nebulizers transform liquid medication into a mist, enabling easier inhalation, especially for those with severe respiratory conditions. Each device plays a crucial role in managing chronic pulmonary diseases effectively.</p></p>
