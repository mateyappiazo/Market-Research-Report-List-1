<p><strong>The Reheating Furnaces Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Walking Hearth Furnaces</li><li>Pusher Furnaces</li><li>Walking-Beam Furnaces</li><li>Refractory Beam Furnace</li><li>Tunnel Furnaces</li><li>Rotary Hearth Furnaces</li><li>Rototherm Furnaces</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Reheating furnaces are essential in metal processing, with various types tailored for specific applications. Walking hearth furnaces use a moving hearth to transport materials, while pusher furnaces utilize a series of pushers for continuous loading. Walking-beam furnaces feature a beam mechanism for efficient material handling. Refractory beam furnaces focus on heat retention with durable linings. Tunnel furnaces allow for continuous processing along a linear path, while rotary hearth furnaces rotate materials for uniform heating. Rototherm furnaces combine rotary and thermal technologies for enhanced efficiency.</p></p>
