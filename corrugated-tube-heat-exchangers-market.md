<p><p>Corrugated tube heat exchangers are categorized into single tube and multi-tube types based on their design and functionality. Single tube heat exchangers feature a single corrugated tube, enhancing heat transfer efficiency due to increased surface area while maintaining compactness. In contrast, multi-tube configurations involve multiple corrugated tubes, allowing for greater heat transfer capacity and versatility in handling varying flow rates and temperatures. Both types are used in HVAC systems, chemical processing, and various industrial applications, optimizing thermal performance.</p></p>
