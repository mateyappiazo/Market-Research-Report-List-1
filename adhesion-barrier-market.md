<p><ul><li>Film Formulation</li><li>Gel Formulation</li><li>Liquid Formulation</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The adhesion barrier market comprises three main types: film formulations, gel formulations, and liquid formulations. Film formulations are solid sheets that prevent tissue adhesion after surgery, offering controlled release properties. Gel formulations provide a viscous consistency that conforms to tissue surfaces, enhancing application ease and effectiveness. Liquid formulations are fluid-based solutions that can be easily applied and penetrate difficult-to-reach areas, ensuring comprehensive coverage. Each type addresses specific surgical needs and improves postoperative outcomes by minimizing adhesion formation.</p></p>
