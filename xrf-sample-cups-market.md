<p><p>XRF sample cups are essential tools in X-ray fluorescence (XRF) analysis for preparing solid, liquid, or powder samples. The market primarily includes two types: single open-ended and double open-ended sample cups. Single open-ended cups have one open end, allowing easy sample loading, while double open-ended cups feature openings at both ends, facilitating quick sample interchange and reducing contamination risks. Choosing between these types depends on the analysis requirements, including sample type, handling convenience, and contamination control needs.</p></p>
