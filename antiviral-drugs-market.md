<p><strong>The Antiviral Drugs Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Reverse Transcriptase Inhibitors</li><li>Protease Inhibitors</li><li>Fusion Inhibitors</li><li>Immune System Modulators</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The antiviral drugs market encompasses various categories, each targeting specific viral mechanisms. Reverse transcriptase inhibitors block the viral replication process, particularly in HIV. Protease inhibitors prevent the cleavage of viral proteins, hindering the formation of new viral particles. Fusion inhibitors obstruct the entry of viruses into host cells, reducing infection rates. Immune system modulators enhance the body's immune response against viral infections, improving overall defense. Together, these drug types play a crucial role in managing viral diseases.</p></p>
