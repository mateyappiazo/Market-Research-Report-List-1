<p><p>Chip resistors, or R-chip resistors, are compact electronic components used for current regulation in circuits. The market is divided into pressure-sensitive and thermosensitive types. Pressure-sensitive chip resistors vary their resistance based on mechanical stress, making them useful in applications like force sensors. In contrast, thermosensitive chip resistors change resistance with temperature fluctuations, allowing them to function in thermal sensing and control applications. Both types cater to specific needs in industries such as automotive, consumer electronics, and industrial automation.</p></p>
