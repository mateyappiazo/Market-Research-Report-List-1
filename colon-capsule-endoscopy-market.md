<p><p>The Colon Capsule Endoscopy market is primarily divided into two types based on the photosensitive chip technology used: CMOS (Complementary Metal-Oxide-Semiconductor) and CCD (Charge-Coupled Device). CMOS chips offer advantages such as lower power consumption, faster processing speeds, and compact sizes, making them suitable for advanced imaging applications. In contrast, CCD chips provide high-quality imaging with superior light sensitivity, which is essential for detailed visualization of the colon. Both technologies play a crucial role in enhancing diagnostic capabilities in gastrointestinal healthcare.</p></p>
