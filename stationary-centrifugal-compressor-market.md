<p><ul><li>Single Stage Centrifugal Compressors</li><li>Multi Stage Centrifugal Compressors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The stationary centrifugal compressor market is divided into two main types: single-stage and multi-stage centrifugal compressors. Single-stage centrifugal compressors contain one impeller and are ideal for applications requiring low to moderate pressure ratios, making them simpler and more cost-effective. In contrast, multi-stage centrifugal compressors utilize multiple impellers to achieve higher pressure ratios, catering to demanding applications in industries like oil and gas, chemical processing, and power generation, where efficiency and performance are critical.</p></p>
