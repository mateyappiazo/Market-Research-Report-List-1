<p><p>Li-Fi technology relies on visible light communication to transmit data, utilizing various market segments. LEDs serve as the primary light source, enabling data transmission through modulated light signals. Microcontrollers facilitate the processing and control of these signals, enabling seamless data transfer and integration with existing systems. Photodetectors are crucial for receiving the modulated light signals and converting them back into electrical data. Together, these components form a cohesive infrastructure that supports high-speed, secure wireless communication through light.</p></p>
